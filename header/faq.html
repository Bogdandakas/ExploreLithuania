Frequently Asked Questions:

Q: What is the official currency of Lithuania?

A: Official currency in Lithuania is Euro (€).

Q: Do I need a passport or ID is enough to travel in Lithuania?

A: ID is enough if you are resident of another EU country.

Q: How are the trips organized?

A: All our trips are organized by private buses.

Q: Are bus tickets prices included in trip price?

A: Yes, bus tickets are included.

Q: What about the meals?

A: Food options are included. Check out trip options to find out more.

Q: Where will I spend the nights during 3 days trip?

A: During our 3 day trip hotels are booked for all our customers.

Q: What items should I bring with me?

A: Bottled water, camera and good mood are highly suggested!

Q:Where is the meeting point at the beginning of excursions?

Excursions start at 6:00 at Vilnius Cathedral square ("Vilniaus Katedra" on the map). You will see our tour guide there.
