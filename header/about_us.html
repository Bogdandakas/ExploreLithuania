About Explore Lithuania team:

We are a young team of passionate individuals. We started our project "Explore Lithuania" in early 2017. During our first year we managed to establish stable and highly immersive trips across Lithuania. We experience deep love for our country and as such an idea to create this project was born.

Our vision:

Our vision is to ensure that any person who seeks to explore and find out more about Lithuania is left satisfied. The trips we offer are immersive and rich in cultural and historical content and as such - are perfect activity for those who want to deepen their knowledge in Lithuania.

Our goal:

To ensure the best experience possible for our customers.

To ensure this we focus on few, but very high quality options. The trips that we suggest provide large amounts of information of architectural and historical value. In addition, customers are able to witness the beautiful sights of pristine lithuanian nature.
